Aditus is a revolutionary new platform bringing the world of luxury to crypto-affluents. Using a uniquely decentralised network, Aditus grants access to all aspects of the luxury lifestyle while guaranteeing privacy. Token Sales will begin in November 2017.

A huge opportunity brewing - crypto-affluents and their desired luxury lifestyle

Skyrocketing crypto-currency values have created a growing new class of affluent individuals – the crypto affluents. Many crypto-affluents desire the luxury lifestyle but many find themselves excluded. This is because traditional luxury merchants do not know how to reach and communicate to the equally insular crypto-currency community. Additionally, very few luxury merchants accept cryptocurrencies as payment.


Founded by luxury and technology veterans, Aditus bridges luxury merchants and crypto-affluents. Integrating smart contracts, payment gateways and extremely strong privacy protection technologies into a single platform, Aditus empowers crypto-users to unlock the luxury lifestyle.

Aditus brings the luxury lifestyle to crypto users

Ordinary Membership (Free)

Simply by entering your preference within the Aditus app, Smart Invitations fitting your preferences immediately appear.

These Smart Invitations are sent directly from luxury merchants, and powered by Ethereum smart contracts.

Uniquely, Aditus’ Smart Invitations allow merchants to offer you reward tokens for visiting their showroom or sharing more details on your preferences. They may even offer you special prices on their products & services.

VIP Membership

For the most discerning of crypto-affluents, Aditus offers a paid membership program with the most sought-after luxury privileges. Think 24-7 concierge service, access to the exclusive establishments, discounted charters for yachts and private jets, invitations to fashion shows, film festivals etc.

These services used to be only available to customers of the most exclusive credit cards and private banks. Aditus draws on decades of luxury experience and brings them into the crypto-world.

Three levels of VIP Memberships are available, and only a limited number of slots are available at any one time.

Decentralised Platform

Unlike other rewards platforms, Aditus does not track and collect user data.

Token Sale

Aditus Tokens Distribution

  • 50% - Available for Token Sale

    Only 1 billion Aditus tokens will be created. 50% of all tokens (500m tokens) offered for sale will be split into 3 sale periods (shown below).

  • 20% - Incentives for Luxury Merchants and Eco-System Participants:

    20% of all tokens (200m tokens) will be used to merchant acquisition initiatives.

    Tokens offered to incentivize merchants will be locked up in smart contracts that only allow the tokens to be used for funding Smart Invitations, and cannot be traded or sold. These tokens will be allocated to jump-start Merchants' use of the Aditus platform.

  • 15% Incentives for Strategic Partners & Token Holders:

    15% of all tokens (150m tokens) will be allocated to user acquisition initiatives like air-drops to token holders, and partnerships with other crypto-currency companies.

  • 15% - Retained for Aditus Team:

    15% of all tokens (150m tokens) will be reserved for the Aditus team to ensure long term interest alignment, and also to attract talent. Vested for a period of 12 months if all planned milestones are met.
