Opportunity brewing: crypto-affluents and their desired luxury lifestyle

Skyrocketing crypto-currency values have created a new class of affluent individuals – the crypto affluents. They aspire to the luxury lifestyle but often find themselves excluded. This is because of the insular nature of the crypto-currency community and traditional luxury merchants who do not yet have the means to reach them. Additionally, cryptocurrencies are not widely accepted as payment.
